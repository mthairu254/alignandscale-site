Who We Are

A Consulting Firm Focused on Institutional Strength

Align & Scale Solutions is a Kenyan management consultancy working with healthcare institutions, NGOs, government agencies, and development-sector organisations. We help organisations build the internal systems, governance structures, and leadership capacity they need to deliver results.

Our work spans strategic planning, compliance readiness, operational improvement, and human resource development. We are engaged by boards, CEOs, and institutional leadership teams who need practical, sector-informed consulting support.

Our Mission

To strengthen healthcare institutions and development organisations through strategic consulting, practical systems, and leadership support that creates lasting impact.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted consulting partner for health-sector and development organisations in East Africa, known for integrity, implementation quality, and institutional impact.

Our Values

What Guides Our Work

1

Integrity

We maintain professional honesty, protect client confidentiality, and align our work with ethical standards.

2

Implementation Quality

We do not deliver reports that sit on shelves. Every recommendation is grounded in what the institution can realistically implement.

3

Institutional Respect

We work within existing structures and respect institutional knowledge, leadership dynamics, and organisational culture.

4

Evidence-Based

Our recommendations are informed by sector data, regulatory standards, and the specific context of each institution.

5

Capacity Building

We aim to leave institutions stronger than we found them. Our work builds internal capability, not external dependency.

6

Accountability

We are accountable to the institutions we serve and to the communities those institutions support.

Our Methodology

From Diagnosis to Lasting Change

We follow a structured approach grounded in institutional understanding, practical implementation, and internal capacity building.

1

Institutional Discovery

We begin by understanding the organisation: its leadership structure, regulatory context, operational strengths, and institutional readiness.

2

Collaborative Design

We work with institutional leadership to design solutions that are practical, realistic, and aligned with organisational priorities.

3

Embedded Implementation

We stay through execution, working alongside institutional teams to embed systems, processes, and practices.

4

Internal Ownership

We build the internal capacity needed for the institution to sustain improvements without ongoing external support.

Our Commitment

We Are Committed to Implementation

We believe consulting should create lasting institutional change, not temporary improvements. Our commitment is to the work, the institutions we serve, and the communities that depend on them.
