Our Services

   

Align & Scale Solutions offers a wide suite of services tailored to the needs of hospitals, clinics, and healthcare start-ups.

       
     

Healthcare Business Development & Strategic Planning

     

We guide healthcare institutions in shaping and implementing long-term growth strategies. Our approach involves conducting in-depth diagnostics, crafting visionary goals, and setting strategic targets. We support facilities in repositioning themselves, responding to market demands, and unlocking revenue opportunities through robust business models and turnaround strategies for underperforming units. We also assist in setting up new service lines, evaluating viability, and creating implementation roadmaps.

   
       
     

Marketing Research & Strategy Development

     

We conduct market analysis, patient feedback studies, and competitor reviews to help our clients understand their target audience and refine their service offerings. Our team designs strategic marketing plans and brand positioning strategies that increase patient flow and build a loyal client base. We also support online and offline campaigns, helping institutions boost their public image and visibility in an increasingly competitive healthcare environment.

   
   
     

Human Resource Management Support

     

We offer end-to-end HR solutions—from workforce planning and recruitment to performance management and policy development. Our services include developing organizational structures, preparing job descriptions, conducting skills audits, and facilitating recruitment processes. We also design performance appraisal systems, manage conflict resolution processes, and conduct HR audits to ensure compliance with labour laws.

   
       
     

Policy Development & SOP Formulation

     

We help facilities build robust operational frameworks by drafting policy documents that govern service delivery, administration, and compliance. We formulate service-specific SOPs for outpatient care, laboratory, pharmacy, finance, HR, and more. These policies and SOPs promote standardization, support training efforts, and are critical in attaining accreditations such as ISO or compliance with Ministry of Health and regulatory bodies.

   
       
     

ISO Certification Readiness

     

We support organizations on their path to internationally recognized standards including:

     
           
  • ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management Systems): Ensures quality control and operational efficiency.
    •        
  • ISO 15189 (Medical Laboratories): Focused on quality and competence in medical laboratories.
    •      
     

We provide gap assessments, documentation support, internal audits, and train teams to meet ISO standards and establish a continuous improvement culture. Our role includes developing QMS documentation, coordinating management reviews, and mentoring internal audit teams.

   
       
     

Executive Coaching for SMEs & Health Institutions

     

We coach founders, administrators, and department heads in leadership, communication, decision-making, and strategy execution. Our coaching programs are tailored to help SMEs and health leaders unlock their potential, address operational bottlenecks, and develop strategic thinking. We use tools such as 360-degree feedback, leadership assessment frameworks, and customized coaching plans.

   
   
     

Operational Audits & Compliance Readiness

     

We assess facility compliance with statutory and regulatory frameworks including SHA (formerly NHIF), KMPDC, NCK, KMLTTB, and others. Our audits identify gaps and operational risks while proposing actionable improvements. We develop audit tools, conduct field assessments, and prepare facilities for external inspections and license renewals. We also assist in drawing compliance action plans and conducting mock audits.

   
       
     

Training & Capacity Building

     

Our capacity building programs are customized to improve service delivery. We provide induction training for new hires, continuous professional development sessions, and tailored modules for support staff. Our training covers clinical support, front office operations, administration, and compliance. We also prepare training manuals, assessment tools, and post-training evaluation reports.

   
       
     

Healthcare Research & Market Analysis

     

We conduct research projects and feasibility studies that inform program design, expansion plans, and investment decisions. We analyse trends, generate reports, and support facilities in data-driven planning and decision-making. Our team offers support in proposal writing, baseline surveys, outcome evaluations, and needs assessments.

   
       
     

Remote Administrative & Technical Support

     

Our retainer services provide clients with access to virtual consultancy support in HR, compliance, strategic planning, and business development. We ensure regular check-ins, documentation reviews, and expert advisory even in the absence of on-site consultants. This model offers continuity, affordability, and access to experienced consultants without long-term staffing commitments.
